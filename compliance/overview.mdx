FlexOrch helps technical teams work with documents that contain personal data, with built-in tooling for KVKK (Turkey), GDPR (EU), NIS2 (EU), and eIDAS (EU) requirements.

## What FlexOrch provides

| Regulation | Support |
|------------|---------|
| **KVKK** | PII detection for Turkish personal data types; audit trail; masking before dataset export |
| **GDPR** | 46 EU/US PII types; Article 30 ROPA record; data minimization via masking |
| **NIS2** | Audit log export (JSON + CSV) for incident documentation |
| **eIDAS** | Electronic signature format detection (XAdES, PAdES, CAdES) in documents |

## Important notes

<Warning>
  FlexOrch is a technical tool, not a legal compliance service. The outputs assist compliance workflows but do not constitute legal advice. Consult your data protection officer (DPO) for regulatory obligations.
</Warning>

## Data handling

- Uploaded documents are stored only for the retention period defined by your plan
- PII is masked before dataset export by default
- All pipeline executions are logged and auditable
- No document content is shared with third parties
