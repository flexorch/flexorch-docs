<Note>
  Team management is available on **Pro and Enterprise** plans.
</Note>

FlexOrch supports multi-member teams scoped to your account. All members share the same workspace — documents, jobs, datasets, and connectors are visible to all members with appropriate roles.

## Roles

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `owner` | Full access, including billing and team management |
| `admin` | Full access except billing |
| `member` | Can upload, process, and export |
| `viewer` | Read-only access |

## Manage your team

Go to **Settings → Team** in the platform to invite members, update roles, and revoke access.

See [Invite Members](/team/invite-members) and [Roles](/team/roles) for details.
